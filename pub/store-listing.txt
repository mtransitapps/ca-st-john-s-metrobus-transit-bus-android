Full description:
This app adds St John's Metrobus Transit busses information to MonTransit BETA.

This app provides the schedule as well as news and service bulletins from www.metrobus.com and @MetrobusTransit on Twitter.

BETA: Thanks for your patience while the last features are being added and the last bugs are being fixed.

Metrobus Transit busses serve St John's, Mount Pearl in Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit BETA app will display busses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit BETA app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by St John's Transportation Commission (Metrobus Transit).
http://www.metrobus.com/insidepages.asp

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-st-john-s-metrobus-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with St John's Transportation Commission (Metrobus Transit).

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.metrobus.com and Twitter
