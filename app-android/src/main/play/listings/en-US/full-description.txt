This app adds St John's Metrobus Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the schedule as well as news and service bulletins from www.metrobus.com and @MetrobusTransit on Twitter.

Metrobus Transit buses serve St John's, Mount Pearl in Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://bit.ly/MonTransitPlay

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by St John's Transportation Commission.
https://www.metrobus.com/html-default/gtfs.asp

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-st-john-s-metrobus-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with St John's Transportation Commission (Metrobus Transit).

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.metrobus.com, Twitter
